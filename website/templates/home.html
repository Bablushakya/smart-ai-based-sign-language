Why We Built This

We believe communication is a fundamental human right. Our platform bridges the gap between the Deaf and hearing communities through technology that understands, translates, and teaches American Sign Language. By making ASL accessible to everyone, we're building a more inclusive world where everyone can communicate beyond words.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is SignSpeak really free to use?

Yes! Our core features including basic lessons and real-time translation are completely free. We believe in making ASL learning accessible to everyone.

What equipment do I need?

All you need is a computer with a webcam and internet connection. Our system works with most modern browsers.

How accurate is the real-time translation?

Our current model achieves 98% accuracy for the 6 basic signs, with continuous improvements being made. You can learn A to Z alphabets sign on learn page.
