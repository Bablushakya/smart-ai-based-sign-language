Our Mission & Vision

Transforming how the world communicates through sign language

Our Mission

To create innovative technology that makes ASL learning and translation accessible, accurate, and free for everyone, breaking down communication barriers between Deaf and hearing communities.

Our Vision

A world where communication knows no boundaries, where technology empowers every individual to connect, learn, and understand sign language effortlessly.

Our Values

Accessibility, innovation, inclusivity, and community-driven development guide everything we do at SignSpeak.

Our Story

SignSpeak was born from a simple observation: while technology has advanced tremendously, communication barriers between Deaf and hearing individuals remained significant.

Our founder, inspired by a close friend in the Deaf community, recognized the need for a solution that could bridge this gap using modern AI and machine learning.

What started as a university project has evolved into a comprehensive platform that helps thousands of people learn and translate ASL in real-time, fostering understanding and inclusion.
